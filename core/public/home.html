Instructions

Input a section of a human DNA genome into the textbox, then hit Submit. Our algorithms identify the gene of interest, find mutations in the gene, and determine if they are potential risk factors for breast cancer. We'll even provide suggested drugs for treatment, specifically for the sarcomas typical of the malformed genes.

Our science is based on published material by some of the world's leading researchers. To learn more, look into "Somatic Mutations and Breast Cancer"

About

DNA2Drug is a powerful tool leveraging precision medicine to help suggest potential medications to patients. We provide a way to translate a patient's DNA sequence into actionable suggestions for care. Our goal is to usher in a new era of digital therapeutics to enhance and build upon existing patient care.
